about Project
Boat Export USA is a platform that specializes in the sale and export of used boats and yachts from the United States to customers around the world. The project combines a user-friendly interface, functional solutions, and international logistics to create a full-fledged service that simplifies the process of purchasing and delivering vessels.

Goals&challenges
Expand the reach and allow customers from all over the world to find and buy boats.
Provide a huge catalog of boat options in different categories and price ranges for different customer needs.To make the process of searching, selecting, and buying a boat as intuitive and convenient as possible.Provide customers with confidence that the company conducts safe and reliable transactions and that the boats offered meet quality requirements and standards.
Solution
The site works with international logistics and offers delivery services, which allows users from different countries to buy ships from the United States. The platform should be available in several languages and support a multi-currency interface.
The use of powerful filters for easy sorting by various parameters (year of manufacture, price, type, etc.), a clear interface, and the ability to instantly contact managers for additional information.Feedback and recommendations from previous customers, documentation confirming the authenticity of the vessels, delivery and service guarantees.Ensuring a stable and scalable infrastructure, fast website loading, and protection against hacking with the help of modern cybersecurity tools.
